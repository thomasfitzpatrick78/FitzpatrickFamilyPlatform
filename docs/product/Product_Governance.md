# Product Governance

**Document Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Active

**Milestone:** Milestone 11

---

## Purpose

This document defines product management governance for the Fitzpatrick Family Platform.

---

## Product Ownership

Product ownership governs product intent, value, backlog ordering, and milestone planning.

Architecture governance remains responsible for architecture decisions, ADRs, consistency, and approval.

Engineering executors implement approved work without making product or architecture decisions.

---

## Backlog Refinement

Backlog refinement shall:

- Confirm product scope.
- Confirm capability and epic placement.
- Confirm source traceability.
- Identify dependencies.
- Identify architecture questions.
- Recommend candidate milestone placement.

---

## Definition of Ready

A backlog item is ready for planning when:

- Capability is identified.
- Feature intent is clear.
- Product ownership is confirmed.
- Dependencies are documented.
- Architecture questions are known or explicitly marked for discovery.

---

## Product Definition of Done

A product item is done when:

- Requirements are approved.
- Architecture is approved when applicable.
- Implementation is complete when applicable.
- Validation evidence exists.
- Documentation is synchronized.
- Product Backlog status is updated.
- Definition of Done is satisfied.
