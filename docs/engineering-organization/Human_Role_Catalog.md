**Document Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Active

**Milestone:** EO-13.0

---

## Purpose

This document defines governed human roles for the Engineering Organization.

---

## Role Catalog

| Role | Primary Responsibility | Authority |
|------|------------------------|-----------|
| Chief Architect | Own architecture direction, review outcomes, and decision quality. | Approves architecture decisions and strategic governance. |
| Architecture Review Board | Review workstream outputs, cross-layer impact, and promotion candidates. | Accepts, rejects, or requests revision for architecture-governed artifacts. |
| Product Owner | Maintain customer value, product priorities, and milestone outcomes. | Prioritizes roadmap and backlog within approved governance. |
| Repository Steward | Protect repository structure, documentation quality, and validation discipline. | Accepts repository-governed artifact updates. |
| Customer Representative | Express household needs and validate practical usefulness. | Confirms whether outcomes deliver immediate customer value. |

---

## Human Judgment Principle

AI roles may produce analysis, implementation, documentation, and evidence. Human roles retain authority for strategy, architecture approval, release judgment, and customer value acceptance.
