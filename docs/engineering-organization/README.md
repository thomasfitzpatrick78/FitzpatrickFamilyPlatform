## Purpose

This governed documentation area establishes the AI Engineering Organization as a first-class product and operating model for the Fitzpatrick Family Platform Engineering Organization.

---

## Foundation

The AI-operated Engineering Organization is the primary governed operating capability. The Fitzpatrick Family Platform is its reference implementation and the shared technical foundation. The Fitzpatrick Family Financial Assistant is the flagship customer-facing application enabled by the organization and Platform.

The Engineering Organization operates across three layers:

| Layer | Responsibility |
|-------|----------------|
| Engineering Organization | Product strategy, governance, roles, delivery model, memory, and operating practices. |
| Shared Platform | Shared governed capabilities, infrastructure registry, automation, observability, and reusable engineering foundation. |
| Customer-Facing Applications | User-facing products and experiences that deliver direct household value, led by the Fitzpatrick Family Financial Assistant. |
