## Purpose

This governed documentation area establishes the AI Engineering Organization as a first-class product and operating model for the Fitzpatrick Family Platform Engineering Organization.

---

## Foundation

The AI Engineering Organization is the flagship product. The Fitzpatrick Family Platform is its reference implementation. The Fitzpatrick Family Financial Assistant is the flagship customer-facing application enabled by the organization and Platform.

The Engineering Organization operates across three layers:

| Layer | Responsibility |
|-------|----------------|
| Engineering Organization | Product strategy, governance, roles, delivery model, memory, and operating practices. |
| Platform | Shared governed capabilities, infrastructure registry, automation, and reusable engineering foundation. |
| Applications | Customer-facing products that deliver direct household value, led by the Fitzpatrick Family Financial Assistant. |
