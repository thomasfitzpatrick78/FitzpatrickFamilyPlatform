# Workstream Continuity Brief Template

**Template Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Template

**Governing Specification:** [Workstream Continuity Brief Specification](../Workstream_Continuity_Brief_Specification.md)

---

## Template Use

Use this template to create a Workstream Continuity Brief only for an approved workstream.

This template is not an Active brief. Only one Workstream Continuity Brief may be Active for a workstream after a populated brief is approved under the governing workstream.

---

## Authority Hierarchy

1. Permanent governance.
2. Approved milestone and architecture artifacts.
3. Approved specification or work package.
4. Active Workstream Continuity Brief.
5. Chat prompt and conversation context.
